Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 7, 2026

Superset is operated by Yizhou Wu. This Privacy Policy explains how Superset collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app.

Information We Collect

We may collect account information such as your email address, profile information you provide, workout preferences, workout history, routines, equipment information, and photos you choose to upload or analyze in the app.

How We Use Information

We use your information to provide and improve Superset, generate personalized workouts, save your preferences and progress, support account features, and respond to support requests.

Photos And Equipment Recognition

If you choose to use camera or photo library features, Superset may process selected photos to identify workout equipment and personalize workout recommendations. You control which photos you provide.

AI Features

Superset may use third-party AI services through secure backend systems to help generate workouts, parse routine descriptions, and recognize equipment. Information you submit for these features may be processed by these services only to provide app functionality.

Service Providers

We use service providers such as Supabase for authentication, database, storage, and backend services. These providers process information on our behalf to operate the app.

Tracking And Advertising

Superset does not sell your personal information and does not use cross-app tracking for advertising.

Data Retention

We retain information while your account is active or as needed to provide the app. You may request account deletion from within the app or by contacting us.

Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated data in the app. You may also contact us at leoyizhou@gmail.com for help with deletion requests.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. No system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children

Superset is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at leoyizhou@gmail.com.
